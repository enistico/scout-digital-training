The training venue

All of these half day courses are held in a dedicated computer training room, conveniently located in the Adelaide CBD.

The IVC (International Visualisation Centre)
19 Young Street
Adelaide, South Australia

Car parking

The IVC is surrounded by carparking options. Undercover car parks or public transport are recommended for the half day training courses, as on-street car parks are limited to 1-2 hours).

Food and drinks (i.e. coffee coffee coffee)

A tea and coffee station, water, biscuits and some fruit are provided during the training session.

The IVC is conveniently surrounded by a number of cafes and restaurants for any other food and drinks, before or after the training session.
