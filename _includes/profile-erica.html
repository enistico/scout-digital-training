About the trainer

Erica has been working in digital for nearly 10 years, and is obsessed with understanding and using data to improve online results.

She is passionate about helping webmasters understand how to best manage their own website content, optimising it for visitors and search engines, and interpreting their metrics to improve performance.

Google has recognised Erica as a Google Analytics and AdWords certified professional, meaning that she has passed multiple exams that assess her product expertise.
