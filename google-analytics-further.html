Course Overview:

The ultimate next step in refining your Google Analytics expertise, this course will go beyond the surface and take your existing knowledge and hone and sharpen it, allowing you to dig up much more valuable insights to better predict your audience and enhance your attraction and retention activities.

This course will cover:

  • segments
  • annotations
  • more complex goals and conversions
  • real time analytics
  • linking with Google Adwords
  • setting up and using custom dashboards
  • targeted campaign tracking using UTM codes
  • filters, keyword tracking, campaign tracking
  • user flow
  • syncing with Google Webmaster Tools
  • multichannel funnel conversions and attribution
  • and so much more!

We’ll also delve deeper in to user behaviour and look at the secondary dimensions of page data and you’ll come away with a clearer picture of where to best spend your time and focus.

Understand – and we mean REALLY understand – your online marketing because understanding is the first step to improvement.

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • modern computer training room
  • individual computers and the necessary internet access and software
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

This training is ideal for:

  • savvy business owners and managers
  • in house or independent marketers responsible for driving traffic to and/or activity on websites
  • web developers who want to a greater understanding of what Google Analytics can do
  • anyone who wants to improve their use of Google Analytics
  • past participants of Intro to Google Analytics.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (generally five to twenty attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.

Course pre-requisites:

  • a business or personal website or blog with Google Analytics installed
  • a minimum of one month’s complete data collected
  • a basic understanding of how to use and interpret Google Analytics’ default reports
  • Introduction to Google Analytics course (recommended)

Please note: This course does not cover eCommerce Google Analytics. Register here to be the first to know when these are offered.
