Introduction to Google Analytics

Course Overview:

Do you want to understand who is visiting your website and what they are doing on it?

Do you log in to your Google Analytics, click around, and either don’t know what you are looking at or lose hours looking at everything but still feel like you haven’t learnt anything of value?

This course is designed to get you familiar with the Google Analytics platform (?? right word ??) and give you the confidence to find and focus on the data that is relevant to your website and business.

This course will cover:

Course Pre-requisites:

A business or personal website or blog with Google Analytics installed, and a minimum of 1 month’s complete data collected.

Who it’s for:

  • Savvy business owners and managers who want to understand what can be tracking on their website, to marry with business goals
  • In-house or independent marketers responsible for driving traffic to and/or activity on websites
  • Web developers who want to understand the activity of visitors on sites they build
  • Anyone who wants to grasp the fundamentals of Google Analytics and understand the basic metrics surrounding the visitation to and activity on your website

Who it’s not for:

People who don’t care about numbers and understanding your historical website data
