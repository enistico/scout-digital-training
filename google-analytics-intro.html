Course Overview:

In this comprehensive workshop, you’ll learn how to get great insight into who is visiting your website, how long for, what pages they are viewing, how they find it and what exactly to do with this information to keep people coming back to your site.

You’ll uncover helpful insights, expert data and plenty of information that you didn’t even know you needed to improve results.

With a focus on clear guidance, practical advice and actionable steps, you’ll come away from this training with renewed and up to date information and knowledge.

Stop guessing and start knowing!

Here’s what you’ll explore during this session:

  • admin tab: your account, property and view
  • reporting tab: your audience, acquisition (including direct, mobile, organic, socially driven and other) and behaviour
  • how people find your website, how long they stay on it and where they leave
  • real time analytics
  • setting up simple goal conversions
  • a basic introduction to using segments
  • adding annotations
  • creating an effective and usable reporting template
  • and so much more!

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • modern computer training room
  • individual computers (PC) and the necessary internet access and software
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Each session is restricted to limited participants (generally five to twenty attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.

Course pre-requisites:

  • a business or personal website or blog with Google Analytics installed
  • a minimum of one month’s complete data collected
