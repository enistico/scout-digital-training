Location

The half day courses are held in a dedicated computer training room, conveniently located in the Adelaide CBD.

It is surrounded by carparking options (undercover car parks or public transport are recommended for the half day training courses, as on-street car parks are limited to 1-2 hours), and cafes and restaurants for pre and post sustenance, though a tea and coffee station, biscuits and some fruit are provided for no extra charge.
